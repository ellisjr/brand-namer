### Step 01b: Territories & Whitespace

**Territories explored internally (8):**
1. Calm Precision -- measurement, instrument, clinical
2. Dark Material -- matte, obsidian, carbon
3. Masculine Heritage -- institutional, old-money
4. Animal/Nature Curator -- creatures with relevant qualities
5. Quiet Discipline -- routine, regiment, method
6. Artisanal Process -- craft, cure, formulation
7. Architectural Structure -- facade, column, edifice
8. Sensory Restraint -- texture, temperature, touch

**Territories dropped (3):**
- "Masculine Heritage" -- too close to Old Spice/Brut territory, risks reading fusty
- "Artisanal Process" -- too close to beard-oil indie brands (Beardbrand, Brickell)
- "Architectural Structure" -- strong but overlaps with "Dark Material" and doesn't differentiate enough

**Surviving territories (5):**

**Territory 1: Calm Precision**
- **Emotional promise:** Measured confidence, expertise without arrogance
- **Semantic field:** Caliber, gauge, meridian, assay, benchmark, standard, grade
- **Phonetic profile:** Crisp consonants, clean vowels, 1-2 syllables, trochaic stress. Competence + Sage sound.
- **Anti-patterns:** Should NOT sound like a lab instrument company or a tech startup

**Territory 2: Dark Material**
- **Emotional promise:** Quiet luxury, visual weight, the matte black bottle given a name
- **Semantic field:** Slate, basalt, carbon, oxide, onyx, graphite, obsidian, char, soot, iron
- **Phonetic profile:** Back vowels (oh, oo), hard consonants (k, t, d), darker/heavier sounds. Ruler sound.
- **Anti-patterns:** Should NOT sound like a construction material supplier or a goth brand

**Territory 3: Animal/Nature Curator**
- **Emotional promise:** Precise, elegant creatures that embody masculine restraint
- **Semantic field:** Hawk, wolf, kestrel, merlin, peregrine, corvid, stag, slate, flint, granite, cedar, birch
- **Phonetic profile:** Strong consonants, concrete imagery, 1-2 syllables
- **Anti-patterns:** Should NOT sound like a hunting brand, a beer, or a truck. Avoid obvious predators (WOLF is taken, LION is too literal)

**Territory 4: Quiet Discipline**
- **Emotional promise:** The daily routine elevated to ritual, consistency as luxury
- **Semantic field:** Regimen, method, ritual, code, doctrine, protocol, cadence, practice, habit, standard
- **Phonetic profile:** Clean, measured, institutional. Sage + Ruler sound.
- **Anti-patterns:** Should NOT sound like a military brand, a workout program, or a self-help app

**Territory 5: Sensory Restraint**
- **Emotional promise:** What premium skincare feels like -- cool, smooth, minimal
- **Semantic field:** Balm, tonic, serum, cool, smooth, matte, haze, still, mist, grey
- **Phonetic profile:** Soft fricatives, liquid consonants, calm energy
- **Anti-patterns:** Should NOT sound feminine, floral, or like a wellness/spa brand
