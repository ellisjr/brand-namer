# Brand Naming Session: Sustainable Streetwear Label

## Brief Summary
- **Category:** Sustainable streetwear (hoodies, tees, accessories)
- **Materials:** Deadstock fabrics, recycled materials, limited-run drops
- **Location:** East London
- **Target:** 18-30, sustainability-conscious but style-first
- **Vibe:** Rebellious, conscious, urban, dark, anti-corporate
- **Competitive Set:** Pangaia, Story MFG, 69 Worldwide, Brain Dead, Stussy
- **Constraints:** Works as a streetwear label tag/embroidery. Preferably one word or short.

## Notes on Naming Strategy

**What works in the competitive set:**
- Pangaia: invented, nature-science hybrid
- Story MFG: honest, craft-forward, abbreviation
- 69 Worldwide: provocative number + scale
- Brain Dead: compound, dark humor, graphic
- Stussy: founder surname, became iconic through repetition

**Where the whitespace is:**
The competitors lean either toward earnest sustainability (Pangaia, Story MFG) or pure irreverence (Brain Dead, 69). The gap is **dark sustainability** - names that feel conscious but never preachy, rebellious but grounded in real material truth. This is why REFUSE, REMNANT, ENDS, and OFFCUT are the strongest - they live in that exact intersection.

**Tag/Embroidery test:**
The best streetwear names work in ALL CAPS on a woven tag and as small chest embroidery. Names under 8 characters with strong consonants win: REFUSE, REMNANT, OFFCUT, VOID, MURK, MOLT, ENDS, LOOM, ASH, DROSS.
