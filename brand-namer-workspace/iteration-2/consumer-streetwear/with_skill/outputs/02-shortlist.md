## Preference Profile Summary

The strongest direction is **one-syllable Anglo-Saxon found words with dark energy and multiple meanings**. The brand's story should be embedded in the word's natural dictionary definition — not explained separately. Words that carry both a material/physical meaning AND a defiant/rebellious second meaning score highest (Scrap, Fray, Pitch, Rue).
