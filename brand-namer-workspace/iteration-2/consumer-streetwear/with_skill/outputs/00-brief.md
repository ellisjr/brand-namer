# Brand Brief: Sustainable Streetwear Brand

## What We're Naming
A sustainable streetwear brand based in East London. Makes limited-run drops of hoodies, tees, and accessories from deadstock fabrics and recycled materials.

## Audience
18-30 year olds who care about sustainability but don't want to look like they shop at a hemp store. Think the intersection of Stussy and Patagonia.

## Brand Personality
Rebellious, conscious, urban, a bit dark, anti-corporate.

## Competitors
- **Pangaia** — eco-materials, science-forward, clean aesthetic
- **Story MFG** — handmade, artisanal, slow fashion
- **69 Worldwide** — genderless, deconstructed, provocative
- **Brain Dead** — graphic art collective, chaotic, subcultural
- **Stussy** — OG streetwear, surf/skate roots, global tribe

## Naming Territories
1. **Salvage Yard** — beauty in the discarded
2. **Urban Grit** — city texture, industrial East London
3. **Defiance / Refusal** — anti-corporate rebellion
4. **Dark Nature / Urban Ecology** — hostile-environment nature
5. **Material / Process** — craft of making from waste

## Competitive Whitespace
No major competitor owns industrial/material vocabulary, dark urban ecology, refusal/dissent language, or found-object/remnant concepts.
