**Dominant patterns:** Soft/approachable masculinity, short casual names, buddy-system branding.
**Overused:** Friendly-guy energy, explicit gender markers, approachable-first positioning.
**Whitespace identified:** Quiet authority, material/craft sophistication, clinical precision without coldness, architectural/structural language. Premium-masculine-serious lane is wide open.

### 5 Naming Territories Defined

1. **The Atelier** -- Material Craft & Formulation
   - Semantic: matte, patina, grain, temper, alloy, forge, render, finish, tincture, tonic
   - Phonetic: Crisp consonants, clean vowels, 1-2 syllables, trochaic
   - Anti: NOT folksy craft, NOT industrial-heavy

2. **Quiet Authority** -- Understated Confidence
   - Semantic: caliber, tenor, stance, bearing, regard, reserve, measure, benchmark
   - Phonetic: Hard consonants + flowing vowels, 2 syllables, authoritative
   - Anti: NOT aggressive, NOT corporate, NOT generic luxury

3. **Surface & Structure** -- Architectural Precision
   - Semantic: grain, contour, facade, relief, stratum, lattice, resin, veneer, polish
   - Phonetic: Hard stops, short vowels, monosyllabic/disyllabic
   - Anti: NOT cold/industrial, NOT construction-site

4. **The Ritual** -- Disciplined Self-Care
   - Semantic: ritual, regimen, practice, method, rite, order, protocol, cadence
   - Phonetic: Measured rhythm, balanced stress
   - Anti: NOT spiritual/wellness, NOT feminine, NOT military

5. **Botanical Masculine** -- Nature Curator (default check applied)
   - Semantic: cedar, slate, basalt, birch, moss, flint, ash, oak, resin, vetiver, bergamot
   - Phonetic: Anglo-Saxon roots, hard consonants, earthy vowels
   - Anti: NOT hippie, NOT spa-day, NOT essential oils MLM
