## Dark Horse Picks

These are unconventional names that could be category-defining if the brand team has appetite for risk:

1. **Sinew** — "We're the connective tissue of your data stack." This is the positioning made literal. It's visceral, memorable, and unlike anything in the iPaaS space.

2. **Polyglot** — "We speak every system's language." For a platform that translates between Salesforce, SAP, Snowflake, and dozens more, this is a powerful metaphor. Developer-friendly (polyglot programming is respected).

3. **Osmosis** — "Data flows naturally." Implies zero-friction, effortless integration. The "no-code" angle is baked into the metaphor — it just happens.

4. **Xylem** — The hidden vascular system of plants. Nobody sees it, but nothing lives without it. Perfect for middleware that's invisible but essential. Deeply ownable.

5. **Quorum** — Distributed systems term (consensus). Implies reliability, agreement, and coordination. Technical audience will appreciate the depth.

## Competitive Whitespace Analysis

|------------|-------------|----------------------|
| MuleSoft | Animal + descriptor (mule = beast of burden) | Avoid animal names |
| Boomi | Invented/playful | Playful space is taken — go authoritative |

| Airbyte | Element + technical (air + byte) | Compound element names feel derivative |
| Workato | Activity + suffix (work + ato) | "-ato" suffix space is taken |
| Tray.io | Object + TLD | Simple object names with .io — avoid this pattern |

**Open whitespace:** Classical/Latin names (Tessera, Pontis, Valence), structural metaphors (Strata, Trestle), and direct technical terms (Conduit, Interop) are all underrepresented in this competitive set.

## Final Recommendation

If forced to pick three to take into trademark screening:

1. **Conduit** — Highest instant comprehension. Strongest "Powered by" grammar. Most likely to be loved by developers AND approved by the C-suite.

2. **Tessera** — Most distinctive and ownable. Beautiful metaphor. Premium enterprise feel without being stuffy.

3. **Valence** — Scientific credibility. Implies natural, chemical-level connection between systems. The AI mapping angle maps well to "valence" as a bonding force.

Runner-up: **Strata** if trademark search shows it's clear in the data/integration category.
