# Brand Naming Session: AI-Native Cybersecurity Platform

## Brief Summary
- **Product:** AI-powered cybersecurity platform (anomaly detection, automated incident response, continuous compliance)
- **Stage:** Series A ($12M raised)
- **Target:** CISOs and security teams at mid-to-large enterprises (500-10,000 employees)
- **Tone:** Authoritative, technically credible, modern but not gimmicky
- **Dual audience test:** Engineers must respect it AND C-suite must feel comfortable putting it in a board deck
- **Competitive set:** CrowdStrike, SentinelOne, Darktrace, Wiz, Orca Security, Palo Alto Networks
- **Positioning:** AI-native, not bolted-on AI; next-gen platform
- **Litmus tests:** Works in "Gartner names [X] a Leader in the CNAPP Magic Quadrant" AND as a CLI command
