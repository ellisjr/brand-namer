# Brand Brief: Artisanal Hot Sauce Brand

## What We're Naming
A small-batch artisanal hot sauce brand based in the Willamette Valley, Oregon.

## The Product
- Fermented hot sauces, aged 6+ months
- Locally-sourced peppers from Willamette Valley farms
- Range: mild smoky chipotle to face-melting Carolina Reaper
- Craft process, small batches

## Target Audience
Food enthusiasts aged 25-45 who:
- Shop at farmers' markets and specialty grocery stores
- Follow food creators on TikTok/Instagram
- Value authenticity, provenance, craft process
- Adventurous palates, willing to explore heat levels

## Brand Personality
- **Craft** — maker energy, hands-in-the-dirt authenticity
- **Authentic** — real ingredients, real process, no shortcuts
- **Bold** — unapologetic about flavor and heat
- **Earthy** — rooted in place, in soil, in terroir
- **Irreverent** — playful wit, not taking itself too seriously despite serious craft

## Archetype Analysis
**Primary: Creator** — maker, artisan, "we build this with our hands"
**Secondary: Outlaw** — irreverent, breaks conventions, refuses to be another generic hot sauce
**Tertiary: Explorer** — rooted in place but adventurous in flavor

## Competitive Landscape
| Brand | Energy | What They Own |
|-------|--------|---------------|
| Yellowbird | Clean, organic, approachable | The "clean condiment" space |
| Secret Aardvark | Quirky, cult, Portland weird | Arbitrary animal + mystery |
| Heatonist | Curated, sophisticated, collector | The "heat sommelier" space |
| Fly By Jing | Cultural, bold, unapologetic | Cultural specificity + premium |
| Cholula | Heritage, Mexican authenticity | Traditional iconography |
| Truff | Luxury, status, Instagram-worthy | Premium positioning, truffle-adjacent |

## What the Name Must Do
1. Stand out on a crowded shelf / farmers' market table
2. Work as an Instagram handle (@brandname)
3. Signal craft without being precious
4. Feel bold and earthy, not corporate
5. Avoid hot sauce naming clichés (fire, burn, flame, blaze, inferno)

## Prototype Target
**Fly By Jing** — the energy we're aiming for. Distinctive, culturally grounded, bold, not generic. Think more this, less Frank's RedHot.

## Anti-Patterns to Avoid
- Generic heat references (fire, burn, flame, blaze, inferno, scorch)
- B2B compound patterns (-works, -craft, -wise, -ware)
- Overly precious artisan clichés (Artisan Fire, Handcrafted Heat)
- Anything that sounds like it could be any hot sauce brand
